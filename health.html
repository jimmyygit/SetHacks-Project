Plant Health

Toxic fertilizers that seem good for plants do more harm than good, polluting rivers and streams around the world!

Make sure to pick out weeds, but be careful! If even some weeds escape your grasp, you can end up with a whole new patch!

Furthermore, make sure to pay close attention to your plants, as they can begin to grow rot, but don't mistake it for a healthy coating of moss!
